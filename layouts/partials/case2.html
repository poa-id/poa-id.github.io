About me

Hi! I'm Peter, a passionate Product Designer with expertise in UI, Branding, and Web Design. I've got a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of design principles, I strive to craft digital solutions that not only look visually appealing but also deliver great functionality.

As a Lead Product Designer, I've mostly worked on designing UI for e-commerce sites, B2B and institutional websites, as well as developing Design Systems. As a freelancer, I've designed UI for an Insurance Broker App and an Event Staffing Platform, in addition to my Branding work.

I have a knack for front-end coding, occasionally I'll code a little project,which enables me to bridge the gap between design and development teams. I have worked with teams in Argentina, Mexico, and the United States. Beyond design, I love a good book, and I'm an avid sports enthusiast, with a particular love for football and surfing.
